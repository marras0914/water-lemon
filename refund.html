Refunds are not issued for charges older than 7 days. You can cancel at any time to stop future charges; cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Log in to your Lemon Squeezy customer portal using the link in your subscription confirmation email. You can cancel your subscription there at any time.

Contact

To request a refund or get help with your subscription, email hello@waterlemon.live. We typically respond within one business day.
