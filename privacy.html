Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Market Stats ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Service at waterlemon.live.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information: Email address and payment information collected at checkout by Lemon Squeezy (our billing provider). We do not store your payment card details.
  • API usage: We log API requests for security and rate-limiting purposes. Logs include your API key and the zip codes requested.
  • Subscription data: Subscription ID and status, provided by Lemon Squeezy via webhook, used to manage your API key.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provision and manage your API key
  • To send your API key and setup instructions after checkout
  • To deactivate your key if your subscription lapses or is cancelled
  • To respond to support requests

3. Data We Do Not Collect

The widget runs in your visitors' browsers and does not collect or transmit any data about your website visitors. We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or fingerprinting.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Lemon Squeezy — processes payments and manages subscriptions. Their privacy policy applies to checkout data.
  • Supabase — hosts our database and API. Data is stored in the US.
  • Redfin — source of market data. No personal data is shared with Redfin.
  • jsDelivr — CDN used to serve the widget JavaScript file.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email and API key for as long as your subscription is active. After cancellation, data is retained for 90 days then deleted upon request.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@waterlemon.live. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

API keys are stored in a secured database with access controls. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email hello@waterlemon.live.
